Article 5 (Rights and Obligations of Information Subject and Legal Representative and How to Exercise Rights)



① The information subject may exercise the right to access, correct, delete, or suspend the processing of personal information with respect to Withlive at any time.
② The information subject may exercise the rights in Paragraph 1 by contacting Withlive's personal information protection manager in writing, by electronic mail, facsimile, etc., pursuant to Article 41, Paragraph 1 of the Enforcement Decree of the Personal Information Protection Act, and Withlive will take measures without delay.
③ The information subject may exercise the rights in Paragraph 1 through a legal representative or delegatee. In this case, a power of attorney pursuant to Form 11 of the Enforcement Rules of the Personal Information Protection Act must be submitted.
④ The right to access personal information and request for correction and deletion may be limited in accordance with Article 35, Paragraph 4 and Article 37, Paragraph 2 of the Personal Information Protection Act.
⑤ The right to correct and delete personal information cannot be exercised if other laws stipulate that the information subject's personal information should be collected.



Article 6 (Measures for Securing Personal Information)

1. Restricting access to personal information Withlive controls access to personal information by granting, changing, and revoking access rights to the database system that processes personal information. It also uses intrusion prevention systems to control unauthorized access from outside.

2. Retention and prevention of falsification of access logs Withlive retains and manages records of accessing the personal information processing system for at least one year or more. However, in the case of adding personal information of more than 50,000 people or processing unique identification information or sensitive information, it retains and manages it for at least two years. It also uses security functions to prevent access logs from being tampered with, stolen, or lost.

3. Technical measures against hacking, etc. Withlive installs and updates security programs to prevent personal information from being leaked and damaged by hacking, computer viruses, and the like. It also establishes and operates a system to monitor and block access from outside by using intrusion detection systems.

4. Access control to unauthorized persons Withlive establishes and operates a separate physical storage area for storing personal information and establishes and operates access control procedures for it.



Article 7 (Installation, Operation, and Refusal of Automatic Personal Information Collection Devices)
